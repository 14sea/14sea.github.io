The world this week Politics
本周的世界 政治

Having seized control of Afghanistan, the Taliban told women to stay at home, sup­posedly for their own safety. America and other countries advised their citizens not to go to Kabul airport because of the imminent threat of a terrorist attack. The Taliban warned that there would be “conse­quences” if American troops remained beyond the evacua­tion deadline of August 31st. Ashraf Ghani, Afghanistan’s former president, turned up in the United Arab Emirates. A group of fighters in the Panj­shir Valley continued to resist the new regime.
在夺取了阿富汗的控制权后，塔利班让妇女呆在家里，据说是为了她们自己的安全。美国和其他国家建议其公民不要去喀布尔机场，因为恐怖袭击的威胁迫在眉睫。塔利班警告说，如果美国军队在8月31日的撤离最后期限之后仍然停留，将会有 “后果”。阿富汗前总统阿什拉夫-加尼出现在阿拉伯联合酋长国。潘杰希尔谷地的一群战斗人员继续抵抗新政权。

The World Bank suspended funding to Afghanistan, in part over concerns that the Taliban would interfere with devel­opment projects aimed at women. The imf has already halted payments to the country. America and other countries froze almost all of Af­ghanistan’s $9bn in foreign reserves. Experts warned of a looming economic crash.
世界银行暂停了对阿富汗的资助，部分原因是担心塔利班会干扰针对妇女的发展项目。国际货币基金组织已经停止了对该国的付款。美国和其他国家冻结了阿富汗几乎所有的90亿美元的外汇储备。专家们警告说，经济崩溃迫在眉睫。

Ismail Sabri Yaakob was sworn in as Malaysia’s prime min­ ister following the resignation of the unpopular Muhyiddin Yassin. Mr Ismail Sabri comes from the same circle of politicians who backed the previous government, which mishandled the pandemic. He was picked by Malaysia’s king, who wants him to face a vote of confidence in parliament.
在不受欢迎的穆希丁-亚辛辞职后，伊斯梅尔-萨布里-亚科布宣誓就任马来西亚总理。伊斯梅尔-萨布里先生来自支持前政府的同一个政治家圈子，前政府对大流行病处理不当。他是由马来西亚国王挑选的，国王希望他能在议会中接受信任投票。

America’s Supreme Court overruled Mr Biden’s decision to allow asylum-seekers at the Mexican border into the United States while their cases are heard. Mr Biden had suspended an order from Donald Trump that directed the asylum-­seekers to wait in Mexico. The court said the order must be reinstated, because Mr Biden’s action was probably “arbitrary and capricious”.
美国最高法院推翻了拜登先生的决定，允许墨西哥边境的寻求庇护者在其案件审理期间进入美国。拜登先生暂停了唐纳德-特朗普的一项命令，该命令指示寻求庇护者在墨西哥等待。法院表示，该命令必须恢复，因为拜登先生的行动可能是 “任意和反复无常的”。

Kathy Hochul was sworn in as the governor of New York state, following the resignation of Andrew Cuomo amid claims of sexual harassment.
在安德鲁-科莫因性骚扰指控而辞职后，凯西-霍楚尔宣誓就职为纽约州州长。

The House of Representatives passed a $3.5trn budget with a provision that avoids a filibus­ter when the spending plan is debated in the Senate. Before the vote the Democratic leadership in the House faced a mini­revolt from party moderates, who were placated with assurances that an infrastructure measure that has bipartisan support will come up for a vote before September 27th.
众议院通过了一项35万亿美元的预算，其中的一项规定避免了在参议院就支出计划进行辩论时出现拉布战。在投票之前，众议院的民主党领导层面临着来自党内温和派的小规模反抗，他们得到了保证，一项得到两党支持的基础设施措施将在9月27日之前进行投票。

At least 20 people died when ﬂoods swept through rural areas in west Tennessee. Flooding also killed at least 20 people in Venezuela.
洪水席卷田纳西州西部的农村地区，至少有20人死亡。委内瑞拉的洪水也造成至少20人死亡。

The left­wing government in Bolivia charged Jeanine Áñez, a former interim president, with “genocide”. The farfetched charge refers to the deaths of 20 protesters, some of whom were supporters of the current government, during clashes with police in 2019. She has been in jail since April on charges of planning a coup against Evo Morales, her predecessor as president, and has been hospitalised recently for hypertension and trying to harm herself.
玻利维亚的左翼政府指控前临时总统珍妮-阿涅斯犯有 “种族灭绝罪”。这项牵强的指控指的是2019年与警察发生冲突时20名抗议者的死亡，其中一些人是现任政府的支持者。她自4月以来一直被关在监狱里，被指控策划针对其前任总统埃沃-莫拉莱斯的政变，最近因高血压和试图伤害自己而被送进医院。

After calling a snap election Justin Trudeau, Canada’s prime minister, faced a double whammy of tightening polls and a barrage of criticism over a slow response to evacuating Canadian interpreters from Afghanistan. Polls suggest that Mr Trudeau’s Liberal Party will narrowly win the election on September 20th. But some are within the margin of error, and the Conservatives have crit­icised him for holding the vote while the country faces a fourth wave of covid-­19. Inflation may also hurt his party.
加拿大总理贾斯汀-特鲁多(Justin Trudeau)在宣布举行紧急选举后，面临着民调趋紧和对从阿富汗撤离加拿大翻译人员反应迟缓的连环批评。民意调查显示，特鲁多先生的自由党将以微弱优势赢得9月20日的选举。但有些是在误差范围内，保守党批评他在国家面临第四波covid-19的时候举行投票。通货膨胀也可能损害他的政党。

An appeals court in Kenya upheld a decision by the High Court blocking a constitutional overhaul supported by President Uhuru Kenyatta. The scheme would have created dozens of new constituencies and several new posts, such as a prime minister. Many saw it as an effort by Mr Kenyatta to make it harder for his estranged deputy, William Ruto, to succeed him next year.
肯尼亚的一家上诉法院维持了高等法院的一项决定，即阻止总统乌胡鲁-肯雅塔支持的宪法改革。该计划将设立几十个新的选区和几个新的职位，如总理。许多人认为这是肯雅塔先生为使他疏远的副手威廉-鲁托明年继任而做出的努力。

Hissène Habré, who ruled Chad from 1982 to 1990, died after contracting covid­19. He was serving a life sentence in Senegal for crimes against humanity. Thousands of people were executed, tortured or jailed under his regime.
1982年至1990年统治乍得的侯赛因-哈布雷（Hissène Habré）在感染丙肝后去世。他因反人类罪在塞内加尔服无期徒刑。在他的政权下，数以千计的人被处决，遭受酷刑或监禁。

Tunisia’s president, Kais Saied, extended his suspension of parliament until further notice, raising concerns about the future of the Arab world’s only real democracy. Last month Mr Saied dismissed the prime minister and assumed executive authority, actions that his opponents called a coup. But the president, who was elected on a promise to clean up corruption, has widespread support.
突尼斯总统凯斯-赛义德(Kais Saied)延长了他对议会的停职，直到进一步通知，这引起了人们对这个阿拉伯世界唯一的真正民主国家的未来的担忧。上个月，赛义德先生解除了总理的职务，并掌握了行政权力，他的反对者称这些行动是政变。但这位因承诺清理腐败而当选的总统得到了广泛的支持。

The share prices of Uber, Lyft and other gig-economy companies wobbled after a judge in California struck down Proposition 22, a voter-approved measure that allows the firms to continue classifying their drivers as independent contractors rather than workers. The judge found that the measure was unconstitutional and unenforceable, but stayed his decision until appeals can be heard.
加利福尼亚州的一名法官驳回了第22号提案，该提案允许这些公司继续将其司机归类为独立承包商，而不是工人，此后，Uber、Lyft和其他演出经济公司的股价出现震荡。法官认为该措施违宪且无法执行，但在上诉审理之前暂缓作出决定。

Providing a contrast to the image of tech companies as fiendish reprobates, Airbnb oﬀered free temporary housing to 20,000 Afghan refugees around the world. The homerental platform and its charity have housed 25,000 refugees over the past four years.
与科技公司被认为是堕落的后裔的形象形成对比的是，Airbnb向世界各地的2万名阿富汗难民提供了免费的临时住房。在过去的四年里，这个房屋租赁平台和它的慈善机构已经为25000名难民提供了住房。

South Africa’s official unemployment rate rose to 34.4%, the highest level since the current method of count­ing joblessness began in 2008. Including those in the labour force who are discouraged from looking for work, the rate rose to 44.4%.
南非的总体失业率上升至34.4%，是自2008年开始采用目前的失业计算方法以来的最高水平。包括那些不愿意找工作的劳动力，该比率上升到44.4%。

South Korea’s central bank raised its benchmark interest rate by a quarter of a percentage point, to 0.75%. It was the first rate increase in almost three years, and comes amid growing consumer debt in the surging economy.
韩国中央银行将其基准利率提高了四分之一个百分点，至0.75%。这是近三年来的首次加息，而且是在经济激增的情况下消费者债务不断增加的情况下进行的。
