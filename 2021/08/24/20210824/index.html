热带风暴格雷斯淋湿了海地，减缓了对8月14日发生的7.2级地震幸存者的救援工作。死亡人数上升到2000多人，这个数字可能会进一步增加。这次地震比2010年估计造成20多万人死亡的那场地震更强。

The prime minister is cutting short his minority govern­ment, which has been in oﬃce for just two years, as he hopes that the electorate will reward his administration’s handling of the covid­19 pandemic. His Liberal Party hopes to win more than the 170 seats neces­sary for an outright majority in the country’s Parliament.
在贾斯汀-特鲁多宣布该国将于9月20日提前举行选举后，加拿大的政治家们开始了竞选活动。这位总理正在缩短他的少数派政府的任期，该政府已经成立了两年，因为他希望选民将奖励他的政府对19型流感的处理。他的自由党希望赢得超过170个必要的席位，以获得该国议会的绝对多数。

法国成为最新一个遭受危险野火袭击的欧洲国家，因为南部海岸附近的大片地区不得不被疏散。希腊、葡萄牙、西班牙和土耳其也都受到了大火的严重影响。

Israeli firefighters battled one of the largest wildfires in the country’s history. The blaze burned thousands of hectares of forest and sent a plume of black smoke over Jerusalem, though no serious injuries were reported. The authorities are unsure if it was caused by arson or negligence. The ﬁre has since been contained.
以色列消防员与该国历史上最大的野火之一作斗争。大火烧毁了数千公顷的森林，并在耶路撒冷上空冒出一股黑烟，不过没有严重伤亡的报道。当局不确定这是否是由纵火或疏忽造成的 纵火还是过失造成的。火灾后来得到了控制。

Assailants on motorcycles killed 37 people in western Niger, the Ministry of the Interior said. It was not clear who carried out the attack, but armed groups linked to Islamic State and al­-Qaeda, two mil­itant groups, have been terro­rising the region. Hundreds of civilians in western Niger have been killed by Islamist rebels this year.
内政部说，骑摩托车的袭击者在尼日尔西部杀害了37人。目前还不清楚是谁发动的袭击，但与 “伊斯兰国 “和 “基地 “这两个激进组织有关的武装团体一直在恐吓该地区。今年以来，尼日尔西部有数百名平民被伊斯兰叛乱分子杀害。

The World Health Organisation said a second case of Ebola may have been detected in Ivory Coast, raising con­cerns that the virus has spread in the country’s economic hub, the city of Abidjan. The ﬁrst case involved a woman who travelled by bus to Abidjan from Guinea, where an out­break of Ebola claimed 12 lives earlier this year.
世界卫生组织说，在象牙海岸可能发现了第二例埃博拉病毒，这使人们担心该病毒已经在该国的经济中心阿比让市蔓延。第一例病例涉及一名从几内亚乘车前往阿比让的妇女，今年早些时候，几内亚爆发的埃博拉病毒夺去了12条生命。

Israeli border police killed four Palestinians during a raid in the occupied West Bank. Ac­cording to Israel, the police came under ﬁre after entering Jenin refugee camp to arrest a member of Hamas, a militant Islamist group. It was one of the deadliest battles in the area in months.
以色列边境警察在被占领的西岸的一次突袭中杀死了四名巴勒斯坦人。据以色列称，警察在进入杰宁难民营逮捕一名伊斯兰激进组织哈马斯的成员后遭到袭击。这是几个月来该地区最致命的战斗之一。

Fuel is scarce in Lebanon, which is mired in an economic crisis. The blast occurred as the army was handing out petrol seized from black marketeers.
黎巴嫩发生的油箱爆炸造成27人死亡，数十人受伤，导致抗议者袭击候任总理纳吉布-米卡提的住所。在深陷经济危机的黎巴嫩，燃料十分匮乏。爆炸发生时，军队正在分发从黑市商人手中缴获的汽油。

What’s in store

In America the giants of the retail sector reported solid results. Walmart’s quarterly revenue for the three months ending in July reached $141bn, a 2.4% increase from the previ­ous year. Sales and foot traﬃc also rose at Target, a rival. However, there are signs that the shopping spree may be coming to an end. The Com­ merce Department said that its measure of national retail sales dropped by 1.1% in July, compared with a month earli­er. Spending fell across many categories, with notable declines in the sales of clothing, furniture and cars.
在美国，零售业的巨头们报告了坚实的业绩。在截至7月的三个月里，沃尔玛的季度收入达到1,410亿美元，比前一年增长了2.4%。竞争对手塔吉特(Target)的销售额和人流量也有所上升。然而，有迹象表明，购物狂欢可能要结束了。美国商务部表示，其衡量的全国零售额在7月份比上个月下降了1.1%。许多类别的消费下降，服装、家具和汽车的销售明显下降。

The inﬂation rate in Britain dropped to 2% in June, com­ pared with 2.5% the month before. But inﬂation is expect­ed to climb in the second half of the year, on the back of rising energy costs and global production bottlenecks.
英国6月份的通货膨胀率下降到2%，而前一个月是2.5%。但由于能源成本上升和全球生产瓶颈，今年下半年的通货膨胀率预计会攀升。

The same factors pushed up consumer­price inﬂation in the European Union. The inﬂation rate there was 2.5% in July, up from 2.2% a month earlier.
同样的因素推高了欧盟的消费价格指数。7月份的通货膨胀率为2.5%，比一个月前的2.2%有所上升。

Japan’s economy grew at an annualised rate of 1.3% in the second quarter. The expansion beat market forecasts and was the result of strong consumer spending and investment. Even so, the rebound is slower than that of other big econo­mies such as America. That is partly because only 41% of Japan’s population has been fully vaccinated. Analysts expect Japan’s growth to be modest for the rest of this year.
日本经济在第二季度的年化增长率为1.3%。这种扩张超过了市场预测，是强劲的消费者支出和投资的结果。即便如此，日本经济的反弹还是比美国等其他大国的经济反弹要慢。这部分是因为日本只有41%的人口已经完全接种了疫苗。分析师们预计，在今年剩下的时间里，日本的增长将是温和的。

bhp, the world’s biggest min­er, announced plans to leave the London Stock Exchange and list only in Sydney. Ken MacKenzie, the chairman, said the move will make bhp’s corporate structure “simpler and more eﬃcient”. The firm is the second­biggest listed in London by market capitalisa­tion. It also announced that it will merge its oil and gas divi­sion with Woodside Petro­leum, an Australian firm.

Glencore, another big miner, acquired a stake in Britishvolt, a startup with plans to build Britain’s first gigafactory to make batteries for electric cars. As part of the deal, Glencore will supply the factory with cobalt, which is used in the batteries. The firm is one of the world’s largest producers of the metal.
另一家大矿商Glencore收购了Britishvolt的股份，这家初创公司计划建造英国第一个千兆工厂，为电动汽车制造电池。作为交易的一部分，Glencore将为该工厂提供电池中使用的钴。该公司是世界上最大的金属生产商之一。

In America road­safety reg­ ulators opened an investiga­tion into Tesla. Since 2018, 17 people have been injured and one killed in crashes involving the carmaker’s partially auto­mated driving systems. The investigation will cover 765,000 cars made by Tesla since 2014. The probe is pri­marily concerned with an apparent inability of Tesla’s autopilot to cope with emer­gency vehicles stopped in the road while attending other accidents.
在美国，道路安全监管机构对特斯拉展开了调查。自2018年以来，在涉及该汽车制造商的部分自动驾驶系统的车祸中，已有17人受伤，1人死亡。这项调查将涵盖特斯拉自2014年以来生产的765,000辆汽车。调查主要涉及特斯拉的自动驾驶系统在处理其他事故时显然无法应对停在路上的紧急车辆。

Nvidia, a big American chip­maker, reported strong results, thanks to surging demand for online services and gaming. Revenues rose to $6.5bn in the second quarter, 68% higher than a year earlier. Net income almost quadrupled to $2.4bn over the same period. The growth was propelled mainly by its data­centre and gaming businesses.
美国大型芯片制造商Nvidia报告了强劲的业绩，得益于在线服务和游戏需求的激增。第二季度的收入上升到65亿美元，比去年同期高出68%。同期的净收入几乎翻了两番，达到24亿美元。增长主要是由其数据中心和游戏业务推动的。

T-Mobile, an American wire­less carrier, said that hackers stole customers’ data from its computer network. The theft aﬀects over 40m current and potential customers. The stolen information includes social­security numbers and driving­licence details.
