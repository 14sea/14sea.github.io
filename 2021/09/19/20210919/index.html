The Biden administration defended its decision to introduce vaccine mandates, after more Republican governors said they would ﬁght them in court. Asa Hutchinson, the governor of Arkansas and an advocate of vaccines, said the government’s order “disrupts and divides” America. With only 54% of the population fully vaccinated, Joe Biden thinks mandates are essential. Companies will have to ensure their employees are either jabbed or tested weekly. All federal workers as well as staff in health facilities that receive government money will have to be vaccinated.
在更多的共和党州长表示他们将在法庭上与之抗争后，拜登政府为其引入疫苗强制措施的决定进行了辩护。阿肯色州州长、疫苗的倡导者阿萨-哈钦森说，政府的命令 “扰乱并分裂 “了美国。由于只有54%的人口完全接种疫苗，乔-拜登认为强制规定是必要的。公司必须确保他们的员工每周接受疫苗注射或测试。所有联邦工作人员以及接受政府资助的医疗机构的工作人员都必须接种疫苗。

In Britain the government reversed course and said that vaccine passports for large events would not go ahead. The roll-out of vaccine boosters for over-50s will start, however, to help reduce pressure on the health service. Jabs will also be offered to 12- to 15-year-olds, a controversial move; the official vaccine committee has said the beneﬁt from a health perspective is too small, but the government insists that inoculations will limit disruption to schools.
在英国，政府反其道而行之，表示大型活动的疫苗护照将不会继续进行。然而，将开始为50岁以上的人推出疫苗加强剂，以帮助减少卫生服务的压力。疫苗接种也将向12至15岁的儿童提供，这是一个有争议的举措；专门的疫苗委员会说，从健康的角度来看，好处太小了，但政府坚持认为，接种将限制对学校的干扰。

Gavin Newsom easily defeated a statewide ballot, bankrolled by conservative groups, to boot him out of office as governor of California. The Democrat said his victory represented a “yes to science” and to vaccines.
加文-纽森(Gavin Newsom)轻松击败了由保守派团体资助的全州投票，该投票旨在将他赶出加州州长办公室。这位民主党人说，他的胜利代表了对科学和疫苗的 “肯定”。

国际刑事法院批准对菲律宾总统罗德里戈-杜特尔特的标志性政策–打击毒品的战争中是否犯有反人类罪进行全面调查。

国际捐助者在日内瓦举行的一次未组织的会议上为阿富汗提供了10亿美元的援助。大部分资金在塔利班接管后被切断了。根据联合国的数据，大约90%的阿富汗家庭粮食不足。

The polls, which may hint at the outcome of the presidential race in 2023, are a blow for Alberto Fernández, the president. Several ministers close to his vice-president, who leads a hard-left faction in Congress, offered to resign.
在阿根廷的初选中，执政的贝隆主义联盟在24个地区中的17个地区赢得的选票少于反对派。这些民调可能暗示了2023年总统选举的结果，对总统阿尔贝托-费尔南德斯是一个打击。与他的副总统关系密切的几位部长提出辞职，而副总统领导着国会中的一个强硬左派。

Mr Claude had accused Mr Henry of involvement in the murder of President Jovenel Moïse, and asked that a judge investigate him. It is not clear whether the prosecutor had the authority to make such a request, or whether Mr Henry had the power to sack him. He also ﬁred the justice minister.

联合国警告说，刚果民主共和国东部的暴力事件有惊人的增长。根据联合国的记录，今年北基伍省和伊图里省的民兵有1200人死亡，1100起强奸案。

Mali’s government is close to reaching a deal to hire 1,000 Russian mercenaries to train its army and protect senior oﬃcials. France, which has a large counter-terrorism mission in the country, has suggested it will withdraw its troops if the deal goes ahead.
马里政府接近达成一项协议，雇用1000名俄罗斯雇佣兵来训练其军队并保护高级官员。在该国有大规模反恐任务的法国表示，如果该交易继续进行，它将撤出其部队。

After 13 months of political paralysis, Lebanon got a new government. It is headed by Najib Mikati, a billionaire businessman who has been prime minister twice before. He inherits a severe economic crisis. The president, Michel Aoun, called on the government to resume talks with the IMF over a bail-out.
经过13个月的政治瘫痪，黎巴嫩有了一个新政府。该政府由曾两次担任总理的亿万富翁商人纳吉布-米卡提领导。他接手的是一场严重的经济危机。总统米歇尔-奥恩（Michel Aoun）呼吁政府恢复与国际货币基金组织的救助谈判。

Experts in America said Iran’s enrichment of uranium to near weapons-grade left it in a position to produce enough fuel for a single bomb within a month or so. Making a warhead would take much longer.
美国的专家说，伊朗将铀浓缩到接近武器级别，使其能够在一个月左右的时间内生产足够的燃料，用于制造一枚炸弹。制造一个弹头则需要更长的时间。

Three former American intelligence operatives admitted conducting hacking operations on behalf of the United Arab Emirates. According to American prosecutors, the men have agreed to pay $1.7m to resolve the charges.
三名前美国情报人员承认代表阿拉伯联合酋长国进行黑客行动。根据美国检察官的说法，这些人已经同意支付170万美元以解决指控。

Naftali Bennett became the ﬁrst Israeli prime minister to visit Egypt officially in over a decade. Mr Bennett met President Abdel-Fattah al-Sisi in the resort of Sharm el-Sheikh.

Norway’s conservative prime minister, Erna Solberg, lost an election and conceded defeat after eight years in oﬃce. It is now expected that the Labour Party will put together a ruling coalition with the help of two other parties.
挪威保守派首相埃尔纳-索尔贝格在选举中失利，在执政八年后认输。现在预计工党将在另外两个政党的帮助下组建一个执政联盟。

Germany’s election campaign headed into its ﬁnal stages, with another televised debate won by Olaf Scholz, the Social Democratic Party’s candidate for chancellor.
德国的选举活动进入最后阶段，社会民主党的总理候选人奥拉夫-肖尔茨赢得了另一场电视辩论。

Russia was also heading to the polls, with three days of voting due to start on September 17th in parliamentary elections. The result, a victory for the Kremlin-backed United Russia party, is not in doubt; the only questions are how much trickery there will be and whether there will be big protests after the vote, as happened in 2011.
俄罗斯也在走向投票站，9月17日将开始为期三天的议会选举投票。结果是克里姆林宫支持的统一俄罗斯党获胜，这一点毋庸置疑；唯一的问题是会有多少诡计，以及投票后是否会像2011年那样出现大规模抗议活动。

Russia began a vast military exercise, Zapad-21, which could involve as many as 200,000 troops, making it the largest such event seen in Europe since the end of the second world war. It has raised hackles in nato and Ukraine, as the exercises are taking place close to the Polish and Ukrainian borders.
俄罗斯开始了一场大规模的军事演习，即Zapad21，可能涉及多达20万军队，使其成为第二次世界大战结束以来在欧洲看到的最大的此类活动。这引起了北约和乌克兰的不满，因为演习是在靠近波兰和乌克兰边境的地方进行的。

Boris Johnson removed Dominic Raab as Britain’s foreign secretary, following the poor timing of Mr Raab’s Greek paddle-boarding holiday, as Afghanistan fell to the Taliban. He now heads the Ministry of Justice. The new foreign secretary is Liz Truss, a rising star in the Tory party.
鲍里斯-约翰逊免去了多米尼克-拉布的英国外交大臣职务，此前拉布先生在希腊划船度假的时机不佳，因为阿富汗沦陷于塔利班。他现在是司法部的负责人。新任外交大臣是利兹-特拉斯(Liz Truss)，她是保守党内的一颗新星。

America’s annual inflation rate stood at 5.3% in August, down slightly from the 13-year highs of the previous two months. There was some evidence that inﬂationary pressures may be levelling oﬀ, such as an easing of prices for used cars, which have driven some of this year’s inﬂation because of supply­chain problems in new­vehicle production. Britain’s inﬂation rate, meanwhile, surged from 2% to 3.2%, partly because of a jump in the cost of eating out, which was artiﬁcially lowered a year ago by a government pandemic scheme that subsidised restaurant meals. In Canada inﬂation rose to 4.1%, an 18­year high.
美国8月份的年度通货膨胀率为5.3%，比前两个月的13年高点略有下降。有一些证据表明，通胀压力可能趋于平缓，例如二手车价格的缓和，由于新车生产的供应链问题，二手车价格已经推动了今年的一些通胀。与此同时，英国的通货膨胀率从2%飙升至3.2%，部分原因是外出就餐的成本急剧上升，一年前，政府通过补贴餐厅膳食的大流行计划，使其成本大幅降低。在加拿大，通货膨胀率上升到4.1%，创下18年来的新高。

In Spain, where consumers’ energy costs have soared, the government wants to seize what it describes as “excess proﬁts” from utility companies to reduce household bills.

The controversial Nord Stream 2 pipeline was completed, allowing Russia to bypass Ukraine and pump more gas to Europe. Russia says it can start delivering gas once the “remaining formalities” are completed.
有争议的北溪2号管道已经完工，使俄罗斯能够绕过乌克兰，向欧洲输送更多天然气。俄罗斯表示，一旦 “剩余的手续 “完成，它就可以开始输送天然气。

The cost of shipping a standard large container is four times higher than it was a year ago for a number of reasons, including congestion at ports. Goldman Sachs expanded its consumer-focused business by agreeing to buy GreenSky, a platform that focuses on providing loans for home improvements, for $2.2bn.
高盛通过同意以22亿美元收购GreenSky，扩大了其以消费者为中心的业务，GreenSky是一个专注于为家庭装修提供贷款的平台。
